Better Living Home Health Care ~ We Love What We Do

Providing You with Quality Care at Home

Better Living Home Health believes everyone should receive quality care with all the comforts of home. We are committed to providing reliable home care with compassion and excellence. We happily assist our clients in maintaining independent living while providing superior care.

Help at home with daily activities bring peace of mind and promote faster healing and can decrease relapse instances for the patient. The majority of our clients thrive when recovering in their own homes, have decreased fall risk, and adapt their recovery plan better at home.

Custom Care Based On Your Needs

Many times, home health care services are prescribed by a physician to help a patient recover after an accident, illness, surgery, or chronic health condition to prevent subsequent hospitalization. Choosing Better Living Home Health to assist with your home care needs means you will receive first class, physician approved, clinical care. We provide the higest standard of care daily for clients who need help managing a chronic condition, or are recovering from a hospital stay, illess, or injury.

Our experience and highly trained Registered Nurses and License Practical Nurses provide a spectrum of quality, compassionate care ensuring both the physical and emotional needs of all clients are met. Our entire Nursing staff has received specialized training the the care of Traumatic Brain Injuries, Spinal Cord Injuries, and Home Ventilators. Our specialized training ensures Better Livin Home Health is an industry leader in supporting a multi-disciplinary approach to our clients Plan of Care. Additional specialization includes the following:

  • Wound Care
  • Intravenous Infusion
  • Wound Vac Management
  • Catheter Management
  • Medication Setup
  • PICC and Central Line Management
  • Vent and Trach Care
