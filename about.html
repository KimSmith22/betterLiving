We Specialize In Spinal Cord and Traumatic Brain Injuries

Since opening our doors, Better Living Home Health has been recognized as a leader in maintaining the highest standard of care within the home care industry. Better Living Home Care provides safe and competant care within the privacy of our client's home.

Our Expertise

Our team is committed to providing quality, compassionate, and competant staff members for all cases. We conduct team meetings prior to the start of each case to create a specialized Care Plan. If the client has a high acuity level, a RN will accompany each new staff member to evaluate their competency on invasive procedures. This ensures we maintain the highest quality of care and our client's state of wellness to the best of our abilities.

Our Mission

Better Living Home Health is dedicated to providing exceptional top of the line in-home care you can depend on. Our services are centered around pinpointing client safety, while at the same time improving overall well being and quality of life. Our employees are screened, certified and trained by simulation at our facility by a Registered Nurse.
